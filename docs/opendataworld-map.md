```text
  ↓

  ↓

  ↓

  ↓
Provenance
  ↓

  ↓

  ↓

  ↓

```

### 2. Governance Plane

The Governance Plane controls safe and accountable use.

```text

  ↓
Ownership
  ↓

  ↓
Policy
  ↓
Access
  ↓

  ↓
Action
  ↓
```

### 3. Analytics Plane

The Analytics Plane converts data into reusable analysis and insight.

```text
Metrics
  ↓
KPIs
  ↓
Reports
  ↓
Dashboards
  ↓
Benchmarks
  ↓
Forecasts
  ↓
Insights
  ↓

```

The Agent Plane enables governed automation and assistance.

```text

  ↓
Tool Permission
  ↓

  ↓
```
